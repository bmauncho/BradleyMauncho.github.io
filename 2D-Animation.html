2D-Animation

Duration : 3 weeks | Platform : Adobe After Effects and Adobe Illustrator | Role : Animating

CADA-HABILIMENTS

Life imitates art, and art imitates life. I set out to develop my talents while emulating art in the following animations. This was done in order to produce visually appealing animations.

NYUMBA

I was learning 2D animation looping using simple shapes and contrast to achieve the required effects in this clip.

PHONE

In Swahili, a house is referred to as a "nyumba." I used rotation and perspective in this animation to give it a feeling of volume and a 3D appearance and feel as a method of exploring new techniques in 2D design.

ABDUCTION

In this animation, both rotation and perspective are used to give the phone a spinning appearance, creating the impression of dimension and depth.
